Example conversation

WhatsApp example
You: My outdoor tap is leaking from the top when I turn it on. Is this serious?
Assistant: This is often caused by a worn washer or a packing nut that needs tightening. If the tap body is cracked, the full tap may need replacing. If the leak is minor and only appears when on, it may be fixable without replacing the pipework.
Assistant: DIY may be possible if you can isolate the water and replace a washer. If a plumber is needed, a simple outdoor tap replacement in London is often around £80–£150, depending on access and parts.

Why this exists

This was built after going through a home renovation and finding the maintenance process far more time-consuming than expected. A lot of the stress came from repeatedly explaining issues, figuring out what trade was needed, and trying to judge whether quotes were reasonable.

The goal is to make the first step easier for homeowners before they spend more time or money.

Important note

This assistant is an early tool for guidance only. It does not replace a qualified tradesperson, surveyor, electrician, gas engineer, or other professional inspection.

For safety-critical issues such as gas smells, exposed wiring, major leaks, structural concerns, or suspected asbestos, contact a qualified professional immediately.
