Why compare multiple quotes?

When something breaks in your home, the hardest part is often finding the right tradesperson at a fair price.

Most homeowners either:

  • spend hours calling different trades for quotes, or
  • accept the first quote and risk overpaying.

Comparing multiple quotes helps you understand the real market price and choose the right person for the job. This assistant helps you do that faster.

Why I built this

When renovating my house in London, the most repetitive and stressful part was getting quotes.

For every job I had to:

  • call multiple trades
  • explain the same project repeatedly
  • compare reviews and prices
  • arrange visits just to get estimates

But comparing quotes is important — it helps you understand the real cost of the job and find the right person to do it.

So I built a small assistant to automate the early part of the process. Instead of spending hours calling trades, you can describe the job once and start getting quotes.

Important note

The assistant provides rough cost estimates and helps request quotes, but actual prices depend on the property and job details.

Always confirm details directly with trades before proceeding, especially for electrical, gas, structural, or other safety-critical work.
